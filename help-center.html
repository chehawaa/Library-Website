Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I access library resources?

You can access our resources via the 'Search' or 'Research Help' sections on the website. Some materials are available for download.

Can I use the library on my mobile phone?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile and tablet devices.

What support is available for beginners?

We provide simple guides, tooltips, and beginner-friendly research help. Visit the 'Research Help' section to start learning.

How does the AI Chatbot work?

The AI Chatbot offers instant assistance for common questions and navigating resources. You can chat anytime via the 'Support' section.
